What's a brand?
A brand allows you to manage and connect one blog or website, one Twitter account, one Facebook page or Facebook group, one Instagram business account, one LinkedIn page (only for Premium accounts), one Google Business Profile location, one Facebook Ads account, one Google Ads account, one TikTok Ads account, one Youtube channel and one Twitch account. A brand usually corresponds with a brand, a product or a company.
